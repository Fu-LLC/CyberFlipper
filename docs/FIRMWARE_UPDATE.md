# Firmware Update Instructions

1. Copy the latest firmware files from `firmware/` to your Flipper Zero.
2. Use qFlipper or the Flipper mobile app to flash `firmware.dfu`.
3. Place `updater.bin` and `update.fuf` on the SD card as instructed.
4. Reboot your Flipper Zero and follow on-screen prompts.

For troubleshooting, see `docs/GETTING_STARTED.md` or join our Discord.
